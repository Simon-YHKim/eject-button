Eject Button gives you a clean exit — a fake incoming call that looks and sounds completely real.

■ HOW IT WORKS
Tap EJECT and your phone rings immediately. Or set a timer (10s / 30s / 1 min / custom) so the call comes in at exactly the right moment. Shake your phone secretly to trigger it without anyone noticing.

The incoming call screen looks identical to a real call. Pick it up, say a few words from the built-in script hints, and walk out. No awkward goodbyes. No explanations.

■ FEATURES
• Instant, timer, shake, and custom delay triggers
• Real ringtone + vibration (uses your phone's default)
• Customizable caller name — set it to Mom, Boss, or anyone
• Script hints during the call so you know what to say
• Call history log
• 7 languages: English, 한국어, 日本語, 中文, Español, हिन्दी

No prank. No joke. A real tool for real social pressure.

■ PERMISSIONS
• Display over other apps — shows the fake call UI on top of whatever you're in (core function)
• Phone state — hides the fake call if a real one arrives
• Contacts — optional, lets you pick a real contact as the fake caller
• Camera — only to blink the flashlight LED on incoming call (no photos or video)

■ PRIVACY
Contact and call data stays entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded.
