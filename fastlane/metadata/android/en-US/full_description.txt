"I really need to leave..." A discreet fake call to escape drinking parties, awkward dates, and unwanted gatherings — without the people next to you noticing.

Work dinners, blind dates, family obligations, hostile meetings — we all have moments when we wish "the phone would ring." Eject Button makes that phone ring.

▸ Four trigger modes: shake / side button / immediate / delayed
▸ Recents thumbnail blocked — even if someone glances at your phone, the fake call is invisible
▸ Randomized phone numbers every time — never suspicious
▸ 7 languages supported — English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Spanish, Hindi

■ Who is this for?
- Office workers escaping forced drinking parties
- Anyone leaving awkward dates or social gatherings naturally
- People declining unwanted plans without confrontation
- Those needing discreet help in unsafe situations (Safety Mode)

■ Why is this different from other fake call apps?
"Invisible to bystanders" is the core principle. 99% of fake call apps are built as pranks — their fake screens leak through Recents, notifications, and multitasking views. Eject Button uses FLAG_SECURE so the fake call never appears anywhere except the active call screen.

Names, phone numbers, and scenario content stay entirely on-device. Analytics data is PII-masked before any external transmission.

For real emergencies, call your local emergency line (911 in US, 112 in EU/Korea, 1366 Korea Women's Hotline). This app is an assistive tool, not a replacement for real emergency services.
