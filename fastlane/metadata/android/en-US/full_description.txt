Stuck in a meeting that should have ended an hour ago?
Trapped at a family dinner you can't leave?
On a date that isn't going anywhere?

Eject Button gives you a clean exit — a realistic-looking fake incoming call you trigger yourself to leave an uncomfortable situation.

■ HOW IT WORKS
Tap EJECT and your phone rings immediately. Or set a timer (10s / 30s / 1 min / custom) so the call comes in at exactly the right moment. Shake your phone secretly to trigger it without anyone noticing.

The call screen is styled to feel familiar so the interaction doesn't look out of place. Pick it up, say a few words from the built-in script hints, and walk out. No awkward goodbyes. No explanations.

Eject Button is for your own self-escape. Don't use it to deceive, defraud, or harm anyone else.

■ FEATURES
• Instant, timer, shake, and custom delay triggers
• Real ringtone + vibration (uses your phone's default)
• Customizable caller name — set it to Mom, Boss, or anyone
• Script hints during the call so you know what to say
• Call history log
• 7 languages: English, 한국어, 日本語, 中文, Español, हिन्दी

■ WHEN PEOPLE USE IT
→ Stuck at work dinners with no polite exit
→ First dates that need to end
→ Sales calls that go too long
→ Awkward family gatherings
→ Uncomfortable one-on-ones

No prank. No joke. A real tool for real social pressure.

■ PERMISSIONS
• Display over other apps — shows the fake call UI on top of whatever you're in (core function)
• Phone state — hides the fake call if a real one arrives
• Contacts — optional, lets you pick a real contact as the fake caller

■ PRIVACY
Your contacts and call history stay on your device — we never upload them.
For product improvement the app uses Google AdMob (ad personalization), Firebase Analytics (app-usage events), and Microsoft Clarity (anonymous session recording). No personally identifying information is collected.
